Our origin

DWINK, launched in 2020, is the innovative Ready-to-Drink division of Freshmate, a trusted name in Ready-to-Eat meals in Pakistan since 2007. We began our journey with the refreshing Basil Seed Drink and have since expanded our lineup to include the delightful Nata de Coco Drink and the hydrating Aloe Vera Drink. Catering to both local and international markets, DWINK is committed to delivering natural and wholesome beverages that refresh and rejuvenate.
